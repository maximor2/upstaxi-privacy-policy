Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 18, 2025

1. Introduction

UpsTaxi ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

To provide our taxi booking services, we collect the following types of information:

2.1. Location Data

  • Precise Location: We collect your exact location to match you with nearby drivers and provide accurate pickup and drop-off services.
  • Approximate Location: Used for finding available drivers in your area.

2.2. Personal Information

  • Phone Number: Required for account registration and communication with drivers.
  • Name: Used for identification and driver-passenger communication.
  • Email Address: Optional, used for account recovery and notifications.

2.3. Device Information

  • Camera: Used to take photos for profile pictures or document verification.
  • Microphone: Used for voice communication with drivers (if enabled).
  • Storage: Used to store app data and cached information.
  • Biometric Data: Used for secure authentication (fingerprint/face recognition).

2.4. Usage Data

  • Ride history and preferences
  • Payment information (processed securely through payment providers)
  • App usage statistics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To connect passengers with drivers and facilitate ride bookings.
  • Location Services: To find nearby drivers and provide accurate navigation.
  • Communication: To enable communication between passengers and drivers.
  • Payment Processing: To process payments for rides securely.
  • Account Management: To create and manage your account.
  • Safety: To ensure the safety of our users and prevent fraud.
  • Improvements: To improve our services and user experience.

4. Data Sharing

We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Drivers: We share your location and contact information with assigned drivers to complete your ride.
  • Service Providers: We may share data with payment processors, mapping services, and other necessary service providers.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS.
  • Secure Storage: Personal information is stored securely on protected servers.
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel have access to your data.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to your personal data.
  • Correction: You can update or correct your information in the app settings.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt-out: You can disable location services and other permissions in your device settings.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information, except where we are required to retain it by law.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
